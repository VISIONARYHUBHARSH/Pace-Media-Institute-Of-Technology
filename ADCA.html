Advance Diploma in Computer Applications (ADCA)

Welcome to ADCA Program

What is ADCA?

Advance Diploma in Computer Applications (ADCA) is a one-year academic program that provides a comprehensive understanding of computer applications and their practical use. It equips students with the skills needed to excel in various IT fields.

Career Opportunities

ADCA graduates can pursue roles in data entry, office administration, customer service, and technical support. With advanced skills, opportunities in web design, accounting, and graphic design also open up.

Course Syllabus

  • Fundamentals of Computers
  • Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Advanced Excel Techniques
  • Tally Prime for Accounting
  • Graphic Designing (Photoshop, Corel Draw, InDesign)
  • Web Development (HTML, CSS, CSS3)
